01 / The Problem

The largest purchase of your life — made blind.

In Nigeria, a first-time buyer pays ₦30M–₦150M for a property they've seen twice. No structural survey. No defect report. No professional inspection of the building they're about to commit their life savings to — because a qualified structural surveyor costs ₦150,000+ and weeks of waiting.

They rely instead on the developer's word, a quick walk-through with a relative, and hope. Three years later, they're dealing with a cracked foundation, failed drainage, or a roof that can't survive a rainy season. The purchase of a lifetime becomes the problem of a lifetime.

This is not a knowledge problem. It's an access problem. And BuildScan AI solves it.

02 / Product

A structural engineer, in your pocket.

BuildScan AI uses computer vision trained on Nigerian building stock to identify structural defects, grade their severity, and produce a professional triage report — in under 10 minutes, from photos you take on your phone.

No structural engineering background required — anyone can use it

03 / How It Works

From driveway snapshot to
signed report — in four steps.

📸
1

Capture

Walk the property and take photos of key structural areas — foundations, walls, roof, drainage, and fixtures. The BuildScan app guides you on exactly where to look.

🧠
2

Analyse

Our computer vision model — trained on 200,000+ annotated images of Nigerian and West African building defects — analyses every photo against 47 defect categories.

📋
3

Report

A structured triage report is generated with defect categories, severity ratings, cost estimates, and recommended actions — ready in under 10 minutes.

✅
4

Escalate

For high-severity findings, we connect you to a verified, licensed structural engineer within 24 hours — for a full professional inspection if needed.
